Our Department

 

Colombo Fire Service Department located in the western province of Sri Lanka, the city of Colombo is the administrative and economic center of the country. About one million people with diverse population and economic gap live an urban life in an area of 37 square kilometers. The city of Colombo consists of historical monuments, Portuguese, Dutch and British architecture, as well as modern style skyscrapers, and the port of Colombo, which is a leading port in South Asia, and a variety of famous local and foreign places.

The Colombo Metropolitan Municipality Fire Service Department carries out its operations with a set of extraordinary responsibilities.

Colombo Metropolitan Municipality Fire Services. Among the responsibilities of the department is fire. Improving regulations, regulating fire safety situations, conducting operational activities, conducting training activities. It is important to carry out preventive activities, to provide humanitarian aid and to build a safe environment for people to live normally

To fulfill these responsibilities, the Colombo Metropolitan Municipality Fire Service Department has a broad vision under the Chief Officer with a headquarters fire service station and five sub-service stations located in the Colombo metropolitan area. With the responsibility of protecting the Parliament of the Republic of Sri Lanka, which is the state interest, these sub-service stations have been established in front of the Parliament of Sri Jayawardenepura and further developing those functions, elite security standby duty services are also carried out.

Hierarchy



 

Administrative Division

This section maintains the personal files of the fire officers, maintains the leave records of the officers, prepares the necessary documents for salaries and other allowances, and integrates each section of the fire department with the Colombo Metropolitan Municipality. Operating under an Assistant Secretary of the Sri Lanka Administrative Service, this section assists the Chief Officer by collecting and analyzing data.

Chief Fire Officer

The fire chief as well as the highest official of the fire service department. Directly responsible for the efficient functioning of the department and supervises all its personnel and activities

Assistant Chief Fire Officer

Creating programs to maintain and improve the fire service, preparing plans to expand the service and taking the initiative of each department to assist the Chief Fire Officer.

Duty Officer

This is not a position but the highest officer on twenty-four hours duty. These officers are entrusted with the creation of schedules and personnel management for firefighting teams. At the end of each duty shift, it should be ensured that there are enough officers for the next shift. Firefighters leave management schedules for each role are Responsibilities of a duty officer. They are responsible for deploying officers twenty-four hours a day and three such officers are on team duty in the Fire Service Department.

Basic Firearms

In the event of a similar incident, the higher officer conducting the operations. Supervises the day-to-day operations and training of the fire service department. He is provided with reports from the first rank infantry and mechanized infantry who have served in the organization and must take administrative decisions for the good of the team. Due to the increased responsibilities, one must have exceptional management skills and coordinate with the people and the media on behalf of the organization.

First Line Artillery

Be responsible for emergency responses to a specific vehicle or specific task, including resource and personnel management. In the absence of primary fire brigade, operations supervision, training of firemen should be responsible for the following activities. Leading the firemen is also a task assigned to him, so he needs a high level of knowledge about leading.

Mechanical Firearms

Responsible for driving and equipment. Responsible for maintenance and operation of fire pumps. This is a highly technical position, and the operation of the vehicle is subject to constant scrutiny of work practices. In cases where there are no first-line firemen, the service of that position will also have to be described, so one must have management and leadership qualities.

Firearms

Any officer in the fire department must enter service as a firefighter and this is the starting position in the department.
Firefighters are directly responsible for firefighting, search, and rescue operations. In cases where there is no mechanized infantry, that position should also be worked on.

Amateur Firearms

After being recruited to the fire department, they are subjected to training and evaluation for a year and are asked whether their character and skills are compatible with the high standards of the fire service. At the end of the training period, after a proper examination, he is recruited as a full-time member of the Fire Service Department. It is reported to the Mayor and the Municipal Commissioner that the training period has been successfully completed in a special march to the Colombo Municipal Council.
